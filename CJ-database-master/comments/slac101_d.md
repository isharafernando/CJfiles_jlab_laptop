## To calculate Q2 and x from W2 and E and (8 deg):

W2=m2 + 2mv - Q2 = m2+2m(E-E') - 4EE' (sin(theta/2))**2
=>E'= (m2+2mE-W2)/(2m+4E(sin(theta/2))**2) 
epsilon=1/(1+2(1+v^2/Q2) (tan(theta/2))**2 )
