## Normalization errors: 
  * 7.6% : proton
  * 7.5% : deuteron
  * 1.4% : d/p ratio

* __Notes:__ 
  * original measurement is cross-section; F2 is derived.
  * "deuteron" is actually F2/2 or sigma/d (_i.e._, per-nucleon F2 or sigma)
